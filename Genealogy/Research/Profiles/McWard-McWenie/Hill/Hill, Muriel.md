**Hill, Muriel Beveridge (1883-1948)**

Muriel was born in Colorado and attended Colorado College in Colorado Springs in 1901.  After graduation she was the principal of a high school in Las Vegas, NM.  She married Edward John McWenie in 1910.

Heart Attack at Polls Kills Woman Voter 

LAS VEGAS, N. M., Nov. 2 (A) -Mrs. E. J. McWenie, 65, prominent Las Vegas resident, former society editor of the Daily Optic and for many years a teacher in the city schools, died today after being stricken with a heart attack while voting in today's election. She was the wife of E. J. McWenie, salesman traveling over New Mexico. Mrs. McWenie came to Las Vegas from Idaho Springs, Colo., in 1907 and was a teacher for 25 years. Besides her husband she is survived by a son, E. J. McWenie, Jr., Tucson, Ariz., and a daughter, Mrs. Henry Hosford, St. Louis.
