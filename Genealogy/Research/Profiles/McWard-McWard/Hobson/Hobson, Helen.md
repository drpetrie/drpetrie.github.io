**Hobson, Helen (1897-1975)**

Born and died in Taylorville. She was the daughter of Truman who was a Mayor of Taylorville at one time.

Taylorville, May 10 -- Elmer J. McWard and Miss Helen Hobson, both of this city. were married here last night at 8:30 o'clock in the home of the bride's parents. Mr. and Mrs. T. E. Hobson on West Market street. Rev. W. S. Rounds officiated. The ring ceremony was employed.

Following the wedding a dinner was served. The couple left on the Wabash at midnight for Chicago where they will spend a few days. Upon their return they will make their home with the groom's parents, Mr. and Mrs. R. J. MeWard. 

The bride has lived here all her life. She attended the ward schools and high school. She studied voice from an instructor from the Millikin University at Decatur and has always been active in choir and recital work. She is a member of the Woman's Glee club. 

Mr. McWard has lived in Taylorville only a few years, coming here from Palmer. He taught schools in Christian county and was recognized as one of the successful teachers. Later he entered the Palmer State bank as cashier and held that position until he came to Taylorville to take a position as bookkeeper at the Farmer's bank.
