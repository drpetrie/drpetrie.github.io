**Logan, Samuel (1838-1926)**

Samuel Logan was a veteran of the Civil War. Born in 1838 in Chambersburg, PA. He moved to Taylorville sometime before 1861. On May 25th 1861 he mustered into the 14th Illinois Infantry which was formed at Jacksonville, Illinois. He was a private in Company H and mustered out on June 6^th^, 1864 right before the 14^th^ consolidated with the 15th  infantry.
