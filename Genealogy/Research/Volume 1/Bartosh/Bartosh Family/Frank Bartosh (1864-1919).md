# Frank Bartosh (1864-1919)

Frank was born August, 2^nd^, 1864 in the village of Neznašov in the Pisek region of the Czech Republic.  He was 7 months old when his family immigrated to America and settled in Iowa.

He married Mary Reha on June 7^th^, 1887.

Frank and Mary had eight children all born in Iowa

Frank moved to North Dakota in 1911

Frank died on July 14^th^, 1919 in Lisbon, North Dakota
