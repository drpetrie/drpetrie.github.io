## C. Timeline of Major Events

**1600s–1700s**

* 1630s: Elder John Strong immigrates from England to Massachusetts Bay Colony.
* Late 1700s: Strong family settles in New York and Pennsylvania frontier regions.

**1800s**

* 1835: John W. Spence born in Pontefract, Yorkshire, England.
* 1839: Ludwig Daubel born in Bonfeld, Württemberg, Germany.
* 1847: Oscar Cooper born in Ceres, Pennsylvania.
* 1864: Frank Bartosh born in Neznašov, Bohemia.
* 1871: Sophia Anderson immigrates from Sweden to the U.S.
* 1873: Charles Daubel born in Bonfeld, Germany.
* 1875: John Petri born in Rumelange, Luxembourg.
* 1881: Helbig and Rudolph families immigrate to U.S.
* 1885: Daubel family emigrates to Hanover, Kansas.
* 1891: Petri family arrives in New York aboard the *Westernland*.
* 1893: Marie Rudolph immigrates to U.S. at age 15.
* 1896: Christian Rudolph dies in Philadelphia.

**1900s**

* 1903: John Petri marries Gertrude Helbig in Minnesota.
* 1913: John Petri dies in logging accident.
* 1919: Frank Bartosh dies in Lisbon, North Dakota.
* 1932: Robert Petrie marries Gladys Cooper.
* 1937: Robert and Gladys move west with their children to Spokane, WA.
* 1937: Herman Daubel marries Thressa Strong.
* 1939–1942: Petrie family lives in Conrad, MT and Wallace, ID.

**1940s–2000s**

* 1946: Gertrude Helbig Petri remarries to Champy Petri.
* 1977: Herman Daubel dies of a heart attack.
* 1997: Robert and Gladys visit family in MN at ages 91 and 80+.
* 2002: Gladys Cooper Petrie dies in Spokane.

**Ongoing Legacy**

* 2022: Descendants reconnect through DNA research and shared photos.
* Present: Family history preserved and retold for new generations.
